Project Overview

Welcome to my portfolio! Here are two projects that showcase my expertise and experience in the field of school library media.

Project 1: Science Expo

I hosted a science expo where my students showcased projects focused on climate change. Their presentations educated the community on important topics like climate justice and tree equity, highlighting the environmental and social impacts of these issues. It was a meaningful event that combined science education with community awareness and advocacy

Project 2: Rain Garden

As part of our environmental efforts, I led my students in designing and planting a rain garden to improve sustainability and promote environmental awareness.
